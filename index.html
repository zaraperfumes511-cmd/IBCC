About Us

Building Bridges,
Creating Opportunities

The Indo Bhutan Chamber of Commerce and Industry (IBCC) is dedicated to advancing trade, investment, and economic cooperation between India and Bhutan.

Built on a foundation of longstanding partnership and mutual trust, IBCC brings together businesses, government stakeholders, and trade professionals from both countries onto a single, collaborative platform to drive meaningful economic engagement.

Registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, IBCC works closely with its members to address critical aspects of cross-border trade, including policy matters, import and export facilitation, transportation, logistics, and operational challenges.

Policy Advocacy

Our Mission

To strengthen and expand a transparent and mutually beneficial Indo-Bhutan trade ecosystem through continuous engagement, strong representation, and close coordination with businesses and government authorities, ensuring smoother and more efficient cross-border trade.
